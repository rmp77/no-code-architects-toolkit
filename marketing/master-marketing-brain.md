## Content Angles Library

### ANGLE 1 — Cost Basis (True Cost Per Call)
Most wholesalers don't know their real cost per motivated seller conversation because costs are spread across dialers, skip trace lists, VA time, and management fees. Make the invisible visible. Always ends with: our price beats theirs.

### ANGLE 2 — The Agency Pitch Bombardment
They get called every day by Google Ads guys, Facebook Ads guys, AI widget guys. Every one of them gets paid whether the phone rings or not. We only get paid when it rings.

### ANGLE 3 — Don't Build the Machine
You can learn ads yourself (3-6 months + mistakes). You can hire an agency ($3-4k/month + 60-90 day ramp). Or you can lease the output of a machine that's already running.

### ANGLE 4 — The Reverse ROI Math
A wholesaler makes $12k/deal. Closes 1 in 10 conversations. Each conversation is worth $1,200 to them. They pay $150 for it. That's an 8x return before the deal closes.

### ANGLE 5 — The Fork in the Road
Targeting wholesalers at the exact moment they're asking "should I start running ads?" The answer is: don't start. Lease ours.

### ANGLE 6 — The Flow Report (City-by-City Video Series)
AI news anchor format. "Good morning [City] wholesalers." Shows real Google Ad CPC for that city. Clicks a live ad on screen. "That's $30. Gone. How many clicks before your phone rings?" Drives back to the Flow Program. Infinitely scalable — every city, every state, same message.

### ANGLE 7 — Agent-Investor Targeting
Many real estate agents also flip and wholesale. One relationship = referral source + direct buyer. Free Zillow walkable video is the door opener. Pay-per-call is the conversation once inside.

### ANGLE 8 — The Partnership Reframe

### ANGLE 9 — Monday Morning Visualization
"What if every Monday you woke up and 2-3 motivated sellers had already called over the weekend?" Paint the picture of the business they want to run.

### ANGLE 10 — One Deal Pays For Everything
One wholesale deal at $12k pays for 80 inbound calls at $150 each. Most close 1 in 10. That means 1 deal funds 8 months of calls. The machine pays for itself.
