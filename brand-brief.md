# Brand Brief

## Business
Inbound pay-per-call service for real estate wholesalers. Sellers call in — no outbound dialing required for the buyer. No contracts, no big deposits, charged only per connected call. Deliberately avoiding the term "live transfers" due to negative industry connotation.

## Target Customer
Early-stage real estate wholesalers — new enough that 1-2 consistent inbound calls would move the needle. They're doing outbound (cold calling, texting) but want something more reliable. They've probably heard horror stories about lead services — bad quality, huge upfront deposits, locked-in contracts.

## Desired Action
Call, text, or DM directly.

## Origin Story / Proof
Generated inbound calls for a cousin who was a roofer — it worked. A colleague saw it and asked for the same thing. It worked again, but cost couldn't get under $200/call at the time. Now it can. First real estate application in progress.

## Differentiators
- Pay only per call (no deposit, no contract)
- No huge upfront commitment
- Inbound calls — seller is already interested
- Price point that beats the market (under $200/call)

## Voice / Vibe
Mix of all: straight shooter, educator, confident, real and honest. Adapt tone to the post angle.
