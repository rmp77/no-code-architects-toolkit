## The Problem (1 short paragraph — make them feel seen)

Most [verticals] rely on referrals, Google Ads, or Yelp — and all three have the same problem: you're paying for visibility, not results. You can spend $1,500/month on ads and have no idea if anyone called, what they said, or whether it was worth it.

Meanwhile, you're ranked [position] on Google Maps in [city]. The traffic is there. The problem isn't awareness — it's conversion.

---

## What Pay-Per-Call Actually Is (The Mechanism — 3-4 sentences)

We run inbound call campaigns targeting buyers in your service area who are already searching for what you do. When a qualified prospect calls in, it routes directly to your phone. You only pay for calls that meet a simple standard: real buyer, real location, minimum call duration. No call = no charge.

You don't touch the marketing. You pick up the phone.

---

## The Promise

**Short-term (30 days):**
You'll receive [X–Y] inbound calls in your first 30 days from buyers in your market actively searching for [service type]. Every call is recorded. You see exactly what came in.

**Long-term:**
A consistent, scalable call pipeline where your phone rings with people who already want what you do — not people you have to convince. You focus on closing and delivering. We handle everything upstream.

---

## The Plan — What Happens After You Say Yes

**Week 1 — Setup**
- We map your service area and target keywords
- Build or optimize your Google Maps profile for your specific vertical
- Set up call tracking number routed to your line
- Configure call filters (duration, area code, service type)

**Week 2 — Launch**
- Campaigns go live
- First calls begin routing to you
- You receive a call log dashboard link — every call visible in real time

**Weeks 3–4 — Optimize**
- We review call recordings and filter quality together
- Adjust targeting based on call quality feedback
- Ramp volume based on your capacity

**Ongoing**
- Weekly call volume report
- Direct access to message us with call quality feedback
- Scale up or pause anytime with 7 days notice

## Who This Works Best For

This works when:
- You have capacity to take more jobs right now (at least 10–15 more/month)
- You're in a service area with active search demand (we'll confirm this before you pay anything)
- You're willing to answer the phone — or have someone who does

This doesn't work if:
- You're at full capacity and can't take more work
- You want to run ads yourself and control the creative
- You want a slow-burn brand play (this is pure volume)

---

## The Investment

**Pay-per-call pricing:**
You pay a flat rate per qualified call. No monthly retainer, no ad spend on top.

A "qualified call" = minimum [X] minutes, real buyer in your service area, asking about the service we agreed to target.

**What you do NOT pay for:**
- Wrong number calls
- Calls under [X] minutes
- Calls outside your service area
- Repeat callers within 30 days

**ROI frame:**
If your average job is worth $[X] and your close rate on inbound calls is [Y]%, you break even after [Z] calls. Everything after that is margin.

*Example: A roofer with a $4,500 average job and a 40% close rate breaks even after 3 paid calls. Call #4 is pure profit.*

## BUILDER NOTES (not in the GDoc — for your reference)

### Customizing by vertical before sending

| Variable | Where to get it |
|----------|----------------|
| Job average value | Ask on first call OR research industry averages per vertical |
| Close rate | Use 30-40% as default if unknown |
| Call volume range | Under-promise: 10-20 calls/month until you have vertical-specific data |
| Service area | Pull from their Google Maps listing |
| Current rank | `{{googleRank}}` from the dashboard |

### Vertical-specific result promises (replace headline)

| Vertical | Proof-of-concept headline |
|----------|--------------------------|
| Plumbers | "10–20 inbound service calls/month from Tampa homeowners already searching" |
| Roofers | "Inbound storm-season and insurance claim calls in [city] — pay per call, not per click" |
| HVAC | "After-hours and emergency HVAC calls in [city] — you only pay when someone calls" |
| Personal Injury Lawyers | "Pre-screened accident victims who called in looking for representation in [state]" |
| Auto Insurance Agents | "X-date shoppers in [city] calling in — pay per qualified call, never per click" |
| Landscapers | "Homeowners in [city] ready to schedule — inbound calls, not form fills" |
| Dentists | "New patient calls from [city] residents already searching for a dentist — pay per call" |
| Electricians | "Emergency and panel upgrade calls from [city] homeowners — pay per qualified call" |

### Proof stack to add once you have client wins

- Screenshot of call log (blur client name, keep call count visible)
- One testimonial with first name, vertical, city, and call volume
- Before/after Google rank for one client (screenshot from dashboard)

### When NOT to send this GDoc

- Email #1 or #2 — too early, no trust baseline yet
- If the prospect hasn't responded to cold outreach — send a shorter bridge email first
- If you haven't confirmed basic service area fit — customize the headline before sending
